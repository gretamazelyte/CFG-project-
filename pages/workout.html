Workouts!

Here are some daily workout and exercising apps to help your body stay healthy

Find your feel good at home!

Keep Up Your Exercise Routine With a Little Help From Edinburgh Leisure.
You can find multiple free virtual Les Mills & Life fitness, home exercise classes on their website.
In addition, they provide all the information how to contact their instructors for further advice or join online class!

Free Yoga Challanges!

Yoga With Adriene Has many amazing 30 day yoga challanges available for free online.
They are easy to follow and she is unpretentious for the most part avoiding
the stereotypical Yoga instructor spiritual speaking style.

Fitness with The Body Coach TV!

Fitness with Joe offers home workouts to everyone, no matter what their fitness levels.
As well as his famous PE everyday, he also has a great range of workouts completely for free!

Lose Weight App for women!

With simple and effective fat burning workouts for women, you can lose belly fat, lose thigh and arm fat.
Follow the 30-day plan and take just a few minutes a day to lose weight and get in better shape!

Parkour youtube tutorials!

From beginner moves to how to train parkour in quarentine this is a slightly more unusual source, But Ronnies free youtube videos are helpful and a fun way to work out!
